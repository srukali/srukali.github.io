FlexAdmin

Web application built with Webpack, AngularJS, and Express.

FlexAdmin is FlexShopper's internal web portal, a major platform built to house all data and workflows for all FlexShopper employees. We started from the ground up with a core team of three, then branched out to manage our own teams for separate modules of the application. I led a team of three as the Product Owner in the development of the Marketing Tools section, overseeing the design of the pages and retrieving requirements from our end-users, the marketing department.

FlexShopper Wallet

iPhone and Android Application built with AngularJS.

The FlexWallet App allows users to submit Personal Shopper requests by quickly snapping a picture of the item they want to purchase. I worked on a team of six as a front end Angular developer, focusing on the menu and 'My Account' pages. This app lead to an increase of about $2,000 in sales per day, and growing.

You can checkout the FlexWallet app here.

Trimark Dorms

Website enhanced with Javascript.

The Trimark Dorms Website needed a major update to continue to appeal to college students and be competitive against other housing options. In my last semester of undergrad, I worked part-time as the solo web developer and designer for this project. This was my first project where I got to experience making large customer-facing changes and A/B testing to see how those changes affected the user's experience.

You can view the dorms website here: Trimark Dorms.
