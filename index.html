About me


I'm Katelyn, a Digital Arts and Sciences (DAS) student at the University of Florida, a slave to my internet connection, and a fervent believer in the Oxford comma. My interests include Human-Computer Interaction, User Experience, Web Development, Mobile Development, Graphic Design, and Product Management. (Pretty much a little bit of everything.)

In case you were interested, DAS is a discipline where computer science and art intersect - the curriculum includes working with 3D modeling and animation software, Java GUI, immersion into video game development, and electives that let a student explore a greater breadth of artistic knowledge on design and aesthetics. I think it's a fantastic and unique program that offers me opportunities I wouldn't have found elsewhere.

In my spare time, which is hard to come by these days, I enjoy baking up some cupcakes, watching an episode of my latest favorite show on Netflix, or tending to my orchid that I'm determined to get blooming again. At nights you may find me hanging out with my ball python Koa, or rather she may be hanging out on me.
